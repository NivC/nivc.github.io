I am looking for motivated students to join my research group. If you are considering working with me, please start by taking a look at my research. I also recommend reading this document, which describes my advising style and expectations.

How to Reach Out

In your first email, mention a research direction you would like to explore together. It does not have to be closely related to my previous work, and shorter is generally better. A CV and transcript are not required, but can be helpful.
